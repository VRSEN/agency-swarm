## Connecting your Agent to Zapier

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Zapier Integration">
    1. In the sidebar, go to **Platforms** and click **New Platform** to start the setup.

    ![Screenshot_2025-05-05_at_23.22.13.png](/images/integrations/zapier-integration/Screenshot_2025-05-05_at_23.22.13.png)

    2. Configure the Zapier integration by providing the necessary details, such as the name of the integration, the associated agent, and the description.

    ![Screenshot_2025-05-05_at_23.23.19.png](/images/integrations/zapier-integration/Screenshot_2025-05-05_at_23.23.19.png)
  </Step> 

  <Step title="Install Zapier App on your workspace">
    Currently, the Agencii app is not available on the Zapier marketplace. However, you can install it by clicking on the **Install** button to receive early access.

    ![Screenshot 2025-02-17 at 15.40.42.png](/images/integrations/zapier-integration/Screenshot_2025-05-05_at_23.26.27.png)

    ![Screenshot 2025-02-17 at 15.29.05.png](/images/integrations/zapier-integration/Screenshot_2025-05-05_at_23.16.03.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
